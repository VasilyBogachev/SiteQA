In Scrum teams take a problem and divide into smaller chunks and run incremental and
iterative method:
For Example:<\/strong>
o Listing the features of website like (Signup, Login, CMS and Payment) etc.
o Team will list all these features in (Product Backlog) and order the list to work on
upper items on priority.
o After prioritizing the items team collaborate in (Sprint Planning) event and select
few top items and added them in (Sprint Backlog).
o Team work on sprint backlog items during (sprint) and at the end product
(increment) was delivered.
o (Increment) for any service or product should be up & running and new
functionalities were ready to go OR something customer can see and use.
